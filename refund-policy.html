Refund Policy

PLEASE READ OUR REFUND POLICY CAREFULLY

At Capital Gain Financial Solutions, we strive to offer advisory services that meet your expectations. Please review the following policy carefully, as it outlines your rights and obligations regarding subscription cancellations and refunds.

Eligibility for Cancellation and Refund

You may request a cancellation of your subscription within 7 days of the subscription activation date. Cancellation requests must be sent from the email address registered with your account to capitalgainfinancialsolution@gmail.com. No refunds will be provided if the service has been availed for more than 7 days.

Refund Process and Amount

Once a cancellation request is received and verified, the subscription will be canceled, and your refund will be processed within 7 working days. The GST portion included in the subscription fee will be deducted first. The refund will be calculated based on the net amount after GST and proportionate to the remaining period of your subscription. If your refund is not received within 7 working days of your request, please contact us at capitalgainfinancialsolution@gmail.com for further assistance.

Important Notice

By subscribing to our services and making a payment on (www.capitalgainfinancialsolution.in), you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to this 7-day refund policy. No cancellations or refunds will be issued beyond the 7-day period.
